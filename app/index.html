Born and raised in the countryside, I moved to LA in 2015 to complete my BA at California State University Northridge, majoring in Multimedia Production. I currently work as a Front-end Developer at META+Lab in Los Angeles County.

I am a passionate, driven, ambitious woman who will stop at nothing to achieve her goals. I firmly believe we are the captains of our own ships and whatever it is we want is achievable as long as we have the passion, drive, and grit to get there and make it happen.

On my free time, you can find me hiking in the mountains, kayaking in the Ocean, or horseback trail riding.

I have traveled to Nicaragua and El Salvador over many summer with a non-profit to build schools and teach english to the impoverished communities. I later traveled alone to Spain and Italy. Through my solo travels I gained a boost of independence, confidence, compassion, and gratitude that has become who I am today.

I have a home base in Los Angeles and the North Bay. I'm always interested in meeting new people with similar interests, as well as business or collaboration opportunities.
