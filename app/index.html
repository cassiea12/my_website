Born and raised in the countryside, I moved to LA in 2015 to complete my BA at California State University Northridge, majoring in Multimedia Production (graduating December 2017). I currently work as a Front-end Developer at META+Lab in a cross-discipline team using Agile methodologies in a Scrum Framework to design and develop responsive, cross-browser compatible, and accessible web applications.

I am a passionate, driven, ambitious woman who will stop at nothing to achieve her goals. I firmly believe we are the captains of our own ships and whatever it is we want is achievable as long as we have the passion, drive, and grit to get there and make it happen.

On my free time, you can find me hiking in the mountains, kayaking in the Ocean, or horseback trail riding

I traveled to Nicaragua and El Salvador for three summers with a non-profit to build schools and teach english to the impoverished communities. I later traveled alone to Spain and Italy. Through my solo travels I gained a new found independence and sense of freedom that has become who I am today. After navigating countries by myself where I don't speak the language and don't know anyone, I feel I can conqueror any challenge that comes my way.
